Let's explore the world of Tim Burton

I'll give you a brief introduction to Tim Burton's classics that you definitely have to watch.

Top three films

Beetlejuice: The spirits of a deceased couple are harassed by an unbearable family that has moved into their home, and hire a malicious spirit (Beetlejuice) to drive them out.

Edward Scissorhands: The story of a young man named Edward, who has scissors for hands. Edward is the man-made creation of an old Inventor.

Corpse Bride: A shy groom (Victor) practices his wedding vows in the inadvertent presence of a deceased young woman, she rises from the grave assuming he has married her.
